- **method:** Four scalar statistics computed on the LP2 stream and on each of 200 control streams, then z-scored against the control band. (1) Total chi2 over all 29 conditional next-rune rows vs the analytic resampler prediction (uniform-over-28 with the current value soft-suppressed): LP2 841.6, control 809.0 ± 39.6, **z=+0.82**. (2) Mean windowed monogram chi2 for windows {50,100,200,300,500}: LP2 24.8–26.2, control 26.4–26.5, **z ∈ [−1.51, −0.61]** — at-dispersion, not under-dispersed. (3) Doublet-gap geometric fit (n=85 gaps): mean_gap z=+0.02, CoV z=−0.26 (≈1, geometric), KS-vs-geometric z=−0.33. (4) Per-page monogram chi2 regressed on page index: LP2 slope −0.0532 (Pearson r=−0.129, p=0.348), control slope +0.0094 ± 0.0610, **z=−1.03** — no fatigue/hand drift.

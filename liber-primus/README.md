# Liber Primus

The runic "First Book" that the 2014 puzzle pointed to as the ongoing challenge. ~58+ pages written in the **Gematria Primus** runic alphabet (each rune maps to a letter *and* a prime number).

This folder holds transcription work, the rune↔letter↔prime table, the status of each page (solved / unsolved), and notes on cipher methods that have cracked the solved pages.

See `research/06-liber-primus-status.md` for the full current-state writeup (populated by the research run).

## Quick status
- A minority of pages are decrypted (e.g. "A Warning", "Welcome", "Some Wisdom", "An End", "Parable").
- The majority remain **unsolved**.
- Solved pages used runic substitution + Atbash + Vigenère/totient-based keystreams + page-shift offsets.
- The unsolved pages have resisted because there is (probably) a running key or method that was never published — and 3301 went silent around 2016.
